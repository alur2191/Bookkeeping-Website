Proficient & Cost-Effective Accounting & Bookkeeping Services

SafeBooks offers complete range of accounting and bookkeeping services to a wide range of industries. Our main goal is to provide customized accounting services that meets your business needs and serves as a key component to sustainable growth focused operation.

Services

Payroll Processing

ADP, Gusto, Paylocity, Paychex, Timekeeping apps, and payroll tax processing

Accounts Payable

Entering invoices, bill payment, 1099 filing and maintaining invoices and W9 forms

Accounts Receivable

Billing, recording of invoices, sending them to your clients, deposits and collection

Bookkeeping

Maintaining financial records, performing bank reconciliations and managing the chart of accounts

Financial Reporting

Weekly financial report overview. Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss, and Cash Flow Statements

Tax & Financial Planning.

Filing forms for LLC, Incorporation, Corporation, C-corp, S-Corp tax returns with the help of a CPA

We perform your accounting and bookkeeping tasks while you focus on running your business.

SafeBooks ensures that all your accounting and bookkeeping tasks are performed on time and according to Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) which is issued by Financial Accounting Standards Board (FASB).

We have experienced bookkeepers with a strong accounting background which make sure that all your financial information is recorded accurately, all your bank reconciliations are completed on a monthly basis and every single transaction is categorized accordingly.

Industries

Logistics

Our team has extended experience in the logistics and ground freight transportation industry. Our specialists will be able to assist and resolve various tasks - invoicing properly documented bills of lading, resolving additional charges on loads such as lumper, fumigation, road service fees or IFTA calculation.

Construction

There are many features that make construction accounting different from other businesses and it a customized procedures is a crucial aspect; retention, job costing, change orders, progress billing, customer deposits apart from regular accounts payable, accounts receivable, payroll and other accounting processes.

Marketing

Accounting in marketing provides a clear and holistic view of a company's financial state by analyzing financial data which helps to increase marketing efficiency and make better decisions. This includes analyzing cash flow, revenue, budgeting and forecasting.

Tech

One major feature that makes technology companies unique is their intangible assets. Tech industry is fast evolving and realizing this, we support these companies with accounting processes such as accounts payable, receivable, collection and how to be compliant with federal, state and local tax planning.

Secure cloud accounting is our priority

Cloud accounting is when all your data and books are stored online with the encrypted information and only users with the login can view the information.

One of our main goals is to keep your accounting data as secure as possible with the help of cloud-based software of your choice. With cloud software your data is stored on a secure server which makes it accessible only for the people who have the access to use it.

The benefits of cloud accounting is that you can access your financial data any time and from anywhere, it is easier to collaborate online with your team, and most of the tedious tasks can be automated with the help of modern technology.
