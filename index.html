We perform your accounting and bookkeeping tasks while you focus on running your business.

SafeBooks ensures that all your accounting and bookkeeping tasks are performed on time and according to Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) which is issued by Financial Accounting Standards Board (FASB).

We have experienced bookkeepers with a strong accounting background who make sure that all your financial information is recorded accurately, all your bank reconciliations are completed in a timely manner and every single transaction is categorized accordingly.

Industries

Logistics

Our team has extended experience in the logistics and ground freight transportation industry. Our specialists will be able to assist and resolve various tasks - invoicing properly documented bills of lading, resolving additional charges on loads such as lumper, fumigation, road service fees or IFTA calculation.

Construction

There are many features that make construction accounting different from other businesses and customized procedures are a crucial aspect for this industry. These procedures include retention, job costing, change orders, progress billing, customer deposits, estimates and many more other processes.

Marketing

Accounting in marketing provides a clear and holistic view of a company's financial state by analyzing financial data which helps to increase marketing efficiency and make better decisions. This includes analyzing cash flow, revenue, budgeting and forecasting.

Tech

One major feature that makes technology companies unique is their intangible assets. Tech industry is fast evolving and realizing this, we support these companies with accounting processes such as accounts payable, receivable, collection and how to be compliant with federal, state and local tax planning.

Secure cloud accounting is our priority

Cloud accounting is when all your data and books are stored online with the encrypted information and only users with the login can view the information.

One of our main goals is to keep your accounting data secure as possible on a reliable cloud-based software of your choice. With cloud software your data is stored on a secure server which makes it accessible only for the people who have the access to use it.

The benefits of cloud accounting include the following: you can access your financial data any time and from anywhere, it is easier to collaborate online with your team, and most of the tedious tasks can be automated with the help of modern technology.
